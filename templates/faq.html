Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Coolshawara.

General

What is Coolshawara?

Coolshawara is a prestige farm management platform designed to bring elegance and precision to modern farming. It provides a suite of tools to simplify farm management, from crop tracking to financial planning.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up from the homepage by clicking the "Sign Up" button and filling out the registration form.

Account & Login

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

Finance & Reports

How do I generate a financial report?

Navigate to the "Reports" section from your dashboard and select the desired report type and date range.
